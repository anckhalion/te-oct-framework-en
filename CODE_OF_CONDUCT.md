# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We commit to a respectful, evidence-oriented, and intellectually honest environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Critique ideas, not people
- Provide evidence for claims
- Be explicit about uncertainty
- Respect cultural, philosophical, and scientific diversity
- Keep collaboration constructive

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Deliberate misinformation
- Repeated bad-faith engagement
- Publishing private data without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions that violate this policy.
Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from collaboration channels.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private maintainer contact request via repository issue with title:
`[private] conduct report`
