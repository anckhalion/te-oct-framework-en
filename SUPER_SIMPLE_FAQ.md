# Super Simple FAQ

## 1) What is this project?

A research framework with documents, theory, and validation assets.

## 2) Is this an AI model I can run immediately?

No. This repository is mainly framework and research documentation.

## 3) Is this medical OCT?

No. Here, OCT means Ordinative Category Theory.

## 4) Is this proving AI sentience?

No. The repository explicitly frames itself as research, not proof of sentience.
